goto/16

Purpose

Unconditionally jump to the indicated instruction.

Note: The branch offset may not be 0. (A spin loop may be legally constructed either with goto/32 or by including a nop as a target before the branch.)

Details

Op & Format Mnemonic / Syntax Arguments
29 20t goto/16 +AAAA A: signed branch offset (16 bits)

Constraints

  • A must point to a valid bytecode instruction inside the current method.
  • A must not be 0.

Behavior

  • The (otherwise invisible) program counter PC is set to the address of the instruction plus the given offset, that is, PC' = PC(goto) + A.
  • Executions resumes at PC'.

Exceptions

None.
